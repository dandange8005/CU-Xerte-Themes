How was the PPIE Training Programme Developed?

The training programme was co-produced with a diverse group of stakeholders, including patients, members of the public, researchers and educators, over a 10-month period.

Module Leadership

Each module was co-led by academic lead(s) and at least one public partner lead. Content contributors included public partners, staff, students, external PPIE partners (e.g. NIHR) and external experts.
