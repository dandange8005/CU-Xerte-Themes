How to use

  • Click the feedback button in the bottom right corner
  • A popover will appear with feedback information
  • Click outside the popover or press Escape to close it
  • The button has a lifting effect on hover

Try it now: Hover over the teal button in the bottom right to see the lifting effect, then click it to see the feedback popover!
