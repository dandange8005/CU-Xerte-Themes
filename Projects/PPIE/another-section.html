PPIE Training Programme Authors and Contributors

This comprehensive resource has only been possible due to the incredible support of colleagues, students and public contributors. We also received a UKRI Impact Acceleration Account Award which supported PPI, external input, and our PPIE Showcase (November 2025).

Staff across the College of Biomedical Life Sciences have also volunteered their time, on top of their existing research and teaching commitments, to contribute - developing content, sharing high quality resources, and sharing their invaluable learning and experiences through 48 informative Learning Case Studies. These have informed the quotes included throughout, top tips, guidance and the Learning Case Study library.

We are incredibly grateful for their willingness to dedicate their time to support our ambition for meaningful, ethical, inclusive and impactful PPIE across the CBLS.
